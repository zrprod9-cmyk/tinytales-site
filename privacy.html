TinyTales does not directly collect personal information from viewers. We publish content on third-party platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) and do not operate user accounts, login systems, or data collection forms.

3. Third-Party Platforms

When you watch our content on YouTube, TikTok, or Instagram, those platforms may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of each platform you use.

4. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Our content is designed to be enjoyed without requiring any personal data from viewers.

5. Analytics

We may use platform-provided analytics (such as YouTube Analytics or TikTok Analytics) to understand how our content performs. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual viewers.

6. Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

7. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
